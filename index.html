Our Story

Family-Owned.
Community-Rooted.

PurePath was founded in 2016 by Rosa and Miguel Flores — a husband-and-wife team who believed that exceptional cleaning shouldn't require a luxury budget. Starting with just three clients in their own neighborhood, they built PurePath on a simple conviction: treat every home as if it were your own.

Today, we serve over 200 households across the metro with the same hands-on attention that Rosa and Miguel brought to that first apartment. Every team member is personally trained, background-checked, and held to the hotel-grade standard that made our reputation. We are not a franchise. We are your neighbors.
